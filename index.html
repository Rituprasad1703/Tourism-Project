Most Popular Destinations in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

Marvel at the timeless beauty of the Taj Mahal, Agra, an architectural masterpiece and symbol of eternal love.

Kerala backwaters

Experience tranquil backwater cruises amidst lush greenery and serene waters in Kerala's picturesque backwaters.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

Discover the spiritual oasis of the Golden Temple, Amritsar, a majestic Sikh shrine radiating tranquility and architectural grandeur.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Explore the ancient city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, where spirituality intertwines with vibrant ghats and timeless rituals along the Ganges.

Mumbai, Maharashtra

Experience the vibrant energy of Mumbai, Maharashtra, a bustling metropolis adorned with iconic landmarks, bustling markets, and Bollywood charm.

Ranthambore National Park, Rajasthan

Embark on an exhilarating safari in Ranthambore National Park, Rajasthan, home to majestic tigers and diverse wildlife amidst historic ruins.

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir

Embark on a mesmerizing journey to Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir, where rugged landscapes meet ancient monasteries and high-altitude adventures await.

Mysore, Karnataka

Discover the regal charm of Mysore, Karnataka, home to opulent palaces, vibrant markets, and cultural splendor steeped in history.

Jaipur, Rajasthan

Experience the royal grandeur of Jaipur, Rajasthan, where majestic forts, palaces, and vibrant markets narrate tales of bygone eras.

Darjeeling, West Bengal

Embark on a journey to Darjeeling, West Bengal, where lush tea gardens, panoramic vistas, and the iconic toy train await.

Rishikesh, Uttarakhand

Discover the tranquil beauty of Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, a spiritual haven nestled in the Himalayas, offering yoga, meditation, and adventure.

Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh

Explore the ancient wonders of Khajuraho, Madhya Pradesh, renowned for its exquisite temples adorned with intricate and sensual sculptures.
